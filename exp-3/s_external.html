Lords Institute of Engineering and Technology - Admission Process

Category A (Convener Quota)

Must have passed Intermediate (10+2) with Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry. Must qualify in TGAPCET (for 1st-year entry) or TGECET (for lateral entry to 2nd year). Muslim Minority students may get full fee reimbursement based on the parent's income certificate.

Category B (Management Quota)

Must have passed 10+2 with Physics, Mathematics, and one other relevant subject. Minimum 45% marks (40% for reserved categories). NRI students are also eligible.
