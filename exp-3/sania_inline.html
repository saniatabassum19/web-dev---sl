Empowering Innovation, Transforming Futures
Lords Institute of Engineering and Technology (LIET), established in 2002, is one of Hyderabad’s premier engineering institutions, committed to academic excellence and holistic development. Accredited with NAAC A+ and recognized for its industry-aligned curriculum, LIET provides students with cutting-edge education, fostering innovation and leadership.

Courses Offered

Lords Institute of Engineering & Technology (LIET) offers B.Tech, M.Tech, MBA, and Diploma programs in CSE, AI & ML, Data Science, ECE, Mechanical, Civil, and EEE, providing industry-focused education for a successful career. These programs are designed to equip students with cutting-edge knowledge and industry-ready skills.
